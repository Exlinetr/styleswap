Frequently Asked Questions

How does StyleSwap work?
StyleSwap uses advanced AI technology to transform your photos with different hair styles. Simply upload your photo, wait for AI analysis, choose from 9 different hair styles (Bob, Pixie, Long Layered, Ombre, Balayage, and more), and unlock the result using credits. The process typically takes 2-3 seconds.
What image formats are supported?
We support JPG and PNG formats. Images should be clear and well-lit for best results. Higher resolution images may take slightly longer to process.
How do credits work?
Each hair style transformation costs 1 credit to unlock. Credits are only deducted when you successfully unlock a result. If an error occurs, your credits are preserved. Is my data secure?
Yes! We take privacy seriously. Your photos are processed securely on our servers. Processed results are stored locally on your device in the app's Documents directory. We do not permanently store your original photos on our servers.
What if processing fails?
If processing fails due to a technical error, your credits will not be deducted. You can retry the process or contact our support team for assistance.
Can I access my transformations offline?
Yes! All your processed transformations are stored locally on your device. You can access them through the Process History section in the side menu, even without an internet connection.

Troubleshooting

App crashes or won't start
Try force-closing the app and reopening it. If the problem persists, restart your device. Make sure you have the latest version of the app installed.
Processing takes too long
Processing typically takes 2-3 seconds. If processing takes longer than 30 seconds, please check your internet connection and try again. If the problem persists, contact support.
Can't purchase credits
Ensure you have a stable internet connection and valid payment method. Check your App Store account settings. If issues persist, contact billing support.
Results not as expected
AI processing results can vary based on image quality and content. For best results, use clear, well-lit photos with good facial visibility. Contact support if you're consistently unsatisfied with results.
Can't see my process history
Make sure you've successfully completed at least one transformation. Process history is stored locally on your device. If you've cleared app data, your history will be lost. Contact support if you believe there's a technical issue.
